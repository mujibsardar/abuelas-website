Abuela's – Delete Account

Last updated: February 10, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Abuela's account and associated data.

Request account deletion

Email us from the email address associated with your Abuela's account:

  • Email: support@abuelas.app
  • Subject: Delete my Abuela's account
  • Include: the phone number on the account (if applicable) and your full name

If you can't email from your account email, include enough information for us to verify ownership.

What we delete

When your deletion request is verified and processed, we delete or de-identify:

  • Your profile information (e.g., name, photo, bio, saved preferences)
  • Your account identifiers used by our app (e.g., email/phone used for login)
  • Your in-app chat access associated with your account (where applicable)
  • Other user-linked data stored for your account that is not required for legal or safety reasons

What we may retain

We may retain certain records for a limited time if needed for: legal compliance, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, safety, or required accounting.

  • Order and transaction records (may be retained for compliance and bookkeeping)
  • Safety/complaint reports and related logs (may be retained for safety and compliance)

Where we retain records, we aim to minimize personal data and/or de-identify it when feasible.

Timing

We typically process verified deletion requests within 30 days. Some data may be retained longer where required for legal or safety purposes as described above.

Partial deletion (optional)

Abuela's does not currently offer partial deletion of specific data categories without deleting the account. If you have a special request, contact us at support@abuelas.app.
